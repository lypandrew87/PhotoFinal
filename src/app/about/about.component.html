Hello There

Life’s big moments are not about the photos, they are about YOU. I go beyond the photos to make the session an experience. For couples, the sessions are an opportunity for open communication with your partner and a time for reflection on the past, present and future of your relationship. We start in a pose that will flatter you in photos, then I ask the bigger questions. What do you love most about your partner? When was the first time you said ‘I love you’ to your partner? What do you most look forward to in your relationship? And don’t worry, I’m not spying on your answers. I merely photograph your private moment together. Rest assured that we’ll have fun too! Dancing, silly photos, and laughing til our tummies hurt are all part of the day as well. If you are an easy going couple that wants to grow in your relationship and have a fun time doing it, I’m just the photographer for you.
